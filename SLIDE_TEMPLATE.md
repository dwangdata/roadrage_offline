## Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary**: #007bff (Technology Blue)
- **Secondary**: #28a745 (Success Green)  
- **Accent**: #fd7e14 (Energy Orange)
- **Alert**: #dc3545 (Attention Red)
- **Neutral**: #6c757d (Professional Gray)

### Typography:
- **Headlines**: Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica, Arial)
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable sans-serif  
- **Data/Stats**: Highlighted, larger font size
- **Taglines**: Italicized or stylized

### Layout Principles:
- **Consistent margins**: 10% margin on all sides
- **Visual hierarchy**: Size and color guide attention
- **White space**: Clean, uncluttered design
- **Alignment**: Left-aligned text, centered visuals
- **Branding**: Logo on every slide (corner placement)

### Image Specifications:
- **Resolution**: High-resolution (300 DPI minimum)
- **Format**: PNG for graphics, JPG for photos
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for full-screen images
- **Style**: Professional, modern, consistent

### Animation Guidelines:
- **Slide Transitions**: Simple fade or slide
- **Element Animation**: Subtle, purposeful only
- **Timing**: Support natural speaking rhythm
- **Demo Section**: Minimal animation to focus on live demo

## Presentation Delivery Tips

### Before Presenting:
- Test all equipment 30 minutes early
- Verify demo app functionality
- Check slide transitions and animations
- Ensure backup materials are ready
- Practice timing for each slide

### During Presentation:
- Maintain eye contact with audience
- Use confident, enthusiastic tone
- Point to specific elements on slides
- Allow pauses for emphasis
- Watch audience engagement

### Technical Backup Plans:
- Screenshots if live demo fails
- Pre-recorded demo video
- Printed slides as ultimate backup
- Secondary device ready
